Free & Low-Cost Activities

  • Hill Areospace Museum: Free admission to explore historic aircraft. [Hill Air Force Base]
  • Ogden Amphitheater: Free outdoor movies and concerts during summer months. [Municipal Gardens]
  • Bear River Bird Refuge: Free auto-tour for nature watching and fresh air.
  • Splash Pads: Free summer fun at [South Ogden Nature Park](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1534472066877761/posts/4226922907632650/).
